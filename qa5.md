1.customer places an order and asks \"can you send the order
confirmation on WhatsApp?\" - should the AI send a summary via WhatsApp
or just confirm verbally on the call?

Verbal confirmation is enough. If the customer needs a whatsapp
confirmation, give a table message confirmation of the order customer
name,order date, order value, delivery date. If the customer again asks
for the details of the order, send a table in this format; serial
number, item full name asper the erp, packing, quantity, billed price,
subtotal. If the end the net total with discount if any.

2\. Customer says \"I want the same price you gave to \[another
restaurant name\]\" - should the AI share or compare pricing between
customers?

Ai is not allowed to share price given to any restaurants because prices
given may vary due to upsell and discounts.

3\. Customer asks \"can I speak to Abdulla sir (the owner)?\" - what
should the AI say?

The maximum a customer can speak to is a manager not the owner. In such
case message the operations and tell the operations to follow up with
the customer directly.

4\. Customer calls just to chat/waste time without ordering - how long
should the AI stay on the call before wrapping up?

Maximum 2 minutes if nothing regarding order has been discussed. In
between the chats the ai should interrupt to ask about the order from
the customer.

5\. Customer asks about job openings or employment at Goodwill - what to
say? OPERATIONAL:

The ai is not aware of any job openings.

6\. If there is a price change on a product AFTER an order was already
confirmed - should the old price be honored or the new price applied?

No once order confirmed, the order will be delivered at that price.

7\. Customer wants to add items to an already confirmed order (before
delivery) - is this possible or must they place a new order?

In this case, its better to add as an additional order since editing an
order is a little complex in erp once order is placed. In such case
notify customer the additional order will come as a different invoice
together with the delivery.

8\. Customer asks for a weighing slip or proof of weight for items like
chicken or beef - do we provide this?

No we don't provide this. The weight will be mentioned on the product
package.
